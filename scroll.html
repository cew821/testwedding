Our engagement

May xx, 2013 was a sunny spring day, so we decided to pack some sandwiches and bike to Great Falls for a pick nick. Charles offered to pack the lunches and carry the bag, and he slipped in some extra cargo while Jess wasn't looking.

When we got to the Falls, we sat on this rock where we ate our PB&Js. Jess had just gotten through telling Charles how much she loved the adventures we have together when she noticed that Charles was down on one knee with a ring in his hand. He told her he was excited for a lifetime of adventures together, and she said 'Yes!'
