Our adventure began in 1983...
Jessica was born on Aug 1, 1983 in Kentucky. Shortly after she moved to Boston with her family
Three weeks later, Charles was born in Minneapolis
18 years later, Jessica graduated high school in Boston and left for Washington D.C. to attend George Washington University. There, she met Charles' cousin Chris
A year later, Charles finished high school and packed his bags for Cambridge, MA to attend college at Harvard
Jessica graduated college, and spent a few years working before moving to Tanzania to attend grad school
Jessica returned from Africa and moved to California, where she attended law school at UC Irvine
Meanwhile, Charles stayed in Boston where he worked as a technology consultant

On a warm summer night in 2010, Charles' cousin Chris visited him in Boston. Chris called some of his old classmates to invite them over for a party. Jessica, in Boston for a legal internship, came over.

Charles and Jessica met that night, watching the sun set over the Charles River.

They have been
together ever since

Our engagement

May 12, 2013 was a sunny spring day, so we decided to pack some sandwiches and bike to Great Falls for a picnic. Charles offered to pack the lunches and carry the bag, and he slipped in some extra cargo while Jess wasn't looking.

At the falls we found a quiet rock by the water to eat our PB&Js. As we watched the waterfall, Jess told Charles how much she loved their adventures. Suddenly, Jess realized Charles was down on one knee with a ring in his hand. He told her he was excited for a lifetime of adventures together, and asked if she would like to marry him...

She said yes!
